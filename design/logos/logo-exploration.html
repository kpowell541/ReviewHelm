ReviewHelm

Logo Exploration
Concept 01
Helm + Code Brackets

Ship's wheel with 8 spokes and handle knobs. Code brackets < > sit in the center hub, directly fusing the "code" and "helm" identities.

Strengths: Immediately reads as "developer tool." Both name words are visually represented.

Best for: Emphasizing the code-focused identity of the app.

Concept 02
Shield + Helm

Purple shield outline containing a white helm wheel. A small checkmark at the shield's base adds a "verified quality" accent.

Strengths: Shield conveys quality gatekeeping. Elegant, professional feel. Two-layer visual hierarchy.

Best for: Positioning as a quality guardian / code protection tool.

Concept 03
Compass + Code

Compass rose with a purple north pointer. A blinking code cursor and forward slash sit in the center. Tick marks and cardinal "N" marker complete the navigation metaphor.

Strengths: Strong learning/journey metaphor. The compass says "I'll guide you." Most unique of the five.

Best for: Emphasizing the learning journey — becoming a better reviewer over time.

Concept 04
Minimal Wheel

Clean 6-spoke helm wheel with bold purple gradient, subtle glow effect, and a white center dot. All-caps "REVIEWHELM" wordmark below.

Strengths: Best scalability — reads clearly at any size. Clean, modern, uncluttered. Most icon-friendly.

Best for: App icon primary use case. Best small-size readability.

Concept 05 — Recommended
Helm + Green Checkmark

8-spoke purple helm wheel with a green checkmark in the center — the universal "PR approved" symbol. Split-color wordmark: "Review" in white, "Helm" in purple. Two-color accent gives energy and distinctiveness.

Strengths: Green checkmark = PR approved. Most visually distinctive. Split wordmark reinforces the two-part name. Combines the helm metaphor with the core review action.

Best for: Standing out in an app drawer. Conveying the satisfaction of a thorough review.
